My Skills

A good user experience begins with good user research. I strive to conduct intentional user research through the lens of diversity and equity to ensure both established target and overlooked groups contribute to the whole picture, yielding enlighening insights that can lead to impactful solutions.

As a champion of the users, it is my belief that if users trust that I am trying to help them solve a need, I will gain key insights that will yield delightful and useful solutions. This involves being relatable and an active listener.

Conducting one on one interviews in a casual conversational manner helps people be their authentic selves, leading to impactful discoveries.
